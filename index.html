About us

Thanks for stopping by The Baking Room!

We're a family-run independent cafe, specialising in freshly baked artisan breads. We also have sandwiches, the odd cake or two and hot drinks. On top of this we can take orders for afternoon teas or celebration cakes. There's plenty of seating inside so come and unwind. Check out our Bakes for a flavour of what we offer.

Opening times
We're open Tuesdays-Saturday 10am till 4pm. Chef Stef bakes fresh a few times a week in our Allandale Road kitchen and we always have sourdough and bread rolls on our menu.

Order ahead
We take pre-orders so Contact us and let us know what you're after. Stef loves to try new recipes so if you have a loaf in mind, get in touch.

Bakes

We love bread and each week Chef Stef meticulously prepares many tasty baked offerings. Sourdough and mini loaves are a staple but keep your eyes out for his specials.
